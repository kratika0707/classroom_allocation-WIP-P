About Us

The Classroom Allocation System offers a variety of features and benefits for students, teachers, and administrators. Students can use the system to view their class schedules and find out which classrooms their classes are held in. Teachers can use the system to schedule their classes and book classrooms. Administrators can use the system to generate reports on classroom utilization and occupancy.
